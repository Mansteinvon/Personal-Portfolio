My vision as an engineer

April 5, 2024 by John Xie

As a child, I harbored ambitions of becoming a military general or political leader, believing that these roles held the power to shape the world. However, with age came the realization that such aspirations were premature and devoid of real meaning. Over time, I came to understand that the path to effecting meaningful change didn't necessarily require grand titles or positions of authority.

My perception shifted when I witnessed the iconic moment of Steve Jobs unveiling a laptop from an envelope. It was then that I recognized the profound impact of engineering on the world stage. I realized that ordinary individuals, through hard work and passion, could wield as much influence as legendary generals or great presidents.

Enrolling in computer science clarified my ideology further. Despite not being astronauts or rocket scientists, engineers are indispensable in endeavors like landing humans on the moon or preserving endangered species. Engineers serve as a gravitational force, drawing in diverse industries and talents. They possess the ability to transform concepts into reality, whether it's generating musical compositions or creating stunning artworks.

As bridges guiding society toward its desired destination, engineers play an integral role in addressing future challenges. With our collective intellect and technological tools, we have the capacity to engineer solutions that benefit the common good. Across different industries and societies, engineers occupy a pivotal position, facilitating progress and innovation. In essence, engineering offers a pathway to mold the world into a better place. With determination and ingenuity, engineers can lead the charge in overcoming obstacles and steering humanity toward a brighter future.
