About Me

April 5, 2024 by John Xie

Hello, I'm John Xie, currently 25 years old and pursuing a degree in Computer Science at York University. Having passed my second year at the institution, I hail from Canton, situated in the southern part of China, near the China South Sea. Cantonese is my mother tongue, and growing up in this vibrant environment, surrounded by talented individuals, instilled in me a deep-seated ambition to continually learn and remain humble in the face of my own ignorance.

To keep pace with my peers, I'm always willing to exert extra effort to achieve my goals. Despite starting with little to no knowledge in a given task, I approach it with confidence, knowing that diligence and perseverance will see me through. While I may not always be the most knowledgeable person in the room, my unwavering passion sets me apart.

Moreover, I embrace my limitations and never shy away from seeking guidance or asking questions. My curiosity drives me to continuously learn and grow, especially in my chosen field of Computer Science. Despite entering post-secondary education at a later age, my dedication and enthusiasm have propelled me to excel in my studies, surpassing even my younger classmates.

Through years of challenges and experiences, I've learned that success is not solely determined by innate talent but by the relentless pursuit of excellence. This belief shapes my approach to every task, whether it's academic assignments, personal projects, or professional endeavors. I am committed to putting forth my best effort and making meaningful contributions in every aspect of my life. I've recognized a significant gap in my communication skills, particularly in articulating the nuances of tasks to my coworkers. This deficiency often requires me to repeat myself multiple times for others to grasp my intended message. I attribute this challenge partially to English not being my native language and to my focus on mathematics and science rather than on developing strong verbal and written communication skills. Understanding the critical role communication plays in effective collaboration, I am proactively addressing this weakness. To enhance my ability to convey information clearly and succinctly, I have devised a strategic plan. Firstly, during my upcoming co-op work term, I intend to engage with at least 10 different team members, ensuring that I communicate my messages with precision and clarity. By seeking feedback from my colleagues, I aim to pinpoint areas for improvement and refine my communication skills. Additionally, I am committed to expanding my exposure to written and verbal communication by dedicating more time to reading novels and newspapers. Through exposure to diverse writing styles and language constructs, I believe I can refine my ability to express ideas concisely and effectively.

I have chosen the picture of the robots that our team made during hackathon ,from no knowledge in robots to be able to make one functional robots, learning is most important aspect for both this race and my life .
